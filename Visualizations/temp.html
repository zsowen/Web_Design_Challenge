Max Temperature

As expected there is a clear correlation between temperature and latitude. The closer to the equator that one gets the more likely the temperature will get. As you get a larger and larger number of degrees north of the equator, you are increasingly likely to find cooler temperatures. Part of this is due to the fact that this data was collected in January, thus the northern hemisphere was in the middle of Winter. The less significant change to the southern hemisphere temperatures is likely due to the fact that it is summer there.
