Humidity

In order to truly gain insight on the effect of latitude on Humidity, more data would need to be gathered. While there does appear to be some concentration of 100% humidity around the equator, and there is a pocket of 0% humidity around the 60-70 degree above the equator latitude, there is not enough to actually discover a significant correlation.
